Who We Are

Our Attorneys

Myers & Shah brings together experienced counsel with backgrounds spanning federal government, Big Four advisory, and major law firms — all focused on serving the needs of DC-area families and individuals.

Mel Myers

Founding Partner

Mel Myers is a founding partner of Myers & Shah, where he focuses on trusts and estates, tax planning, and trust and estate litigation. He counsels individuals and families across the DC metropolitan area on estate planning strategies, tax-efficient wealth transfer, and the resolution of contested estate and trust matters. An accomplished public speaker, Mel has presented at the American Bar Association and state bar events on tax and litigation topics.

Ghezal Shah Myers

Founding Partner

Ghezal Shah Myers is a founding partner of Myers & Shah, where she focuses on land use and zoning and trusts and estates. She is admitted to practice before the United States Supreme Court, the Supreme Court of Virginia, and the District of Columbia Court of Appeals. She provides practical, client-centered counsel to individuals, families, and property owners throughout the Washington, DC metropolitan area.

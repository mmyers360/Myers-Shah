Why Myers & Shah

Project Knowledge,
Across Every Discipline

Real estate and land use work rarely sit in a single silo. Entitlements turn on zoning law, financing turns on tax structure, and disputes turn on the underlying lease or development agreement. Our partners work together across all of those disciplines.

The result is counsel that anticipates how decisions in one phase shape outcomes in the next — from the first zoning meeting through closing, occupancy, and any litigation that follows.
